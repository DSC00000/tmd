Let us help you work smarter.

Code Reader

Each machine is ready to scan QR codes importing data from your shop floor work orders.

Power Options

Every machine is designed to be able to operate on either single or three-phase AC power.

Precision and Accuracy

All TMD machines utilize C# generated logic to control multiple servos, all moving through precision ball screws and timing belts with THK linear bearings throughout ensuring high speed, high accuracy operation.

Satefy Interlocks

Machines are by default equipped with multiple safety features - including light curtains, emergency stops and protective cover relays.

Durability

Each machine's HMI is a ruggedized PC manufactured with industrial use in mind: dirt, moisture, impact resistant.

Support

The TMD service team is dedicated to providing you with remote or onsite customer service to fulfill all your training, repair, parts sales or troubleshooting needs.
